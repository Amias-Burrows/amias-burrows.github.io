Meet Amias

Who is Amias?

Amias Burrows, a Computer Scientist/Musician growing up in rural Lincolnshire, has always had a passion for creating things, whether that be playing with lego when he was younger, to writing original songs with his band and performing them to crouds. He has always stretched his creative legs whenever the occasion presented itself. In college, whilst thinking about considering Engineering, Amias took a course in Computer Science, and after two years learning to code complex programs and algorithms, decided he would prefer to take the more computer sided approach. His background in drumming also led him to complete a two year course as a Music Practitioner. This kickstarted his other hobby, making music. These two combined, keep Amias busy being productive and expanding his knowledge.

What drives him

What has inspired Amias to want to live like this? Is it the constant thought that what he creates might one day change someones life? Is it the thrill he gets from making what he wants and getting the desired outcome? Is it that he loves problem solving issues which arise or just problems he's been tasked with fixing?

well, it's all of them.
Everything I just listed is the reason he loves to focus on his work, no matter what he's doing. This makes Amias the best person to hire for any situation, don't shy away from problems. He sorts them out.
