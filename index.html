Meet Amias

Amias Burrows is a young adult just beginning his career in the music & computing industry. Living in the Lincolnshire Countryside, Amias has always had a passion for creating things. From playing with Lego as a child, to writing original songs with his band Indigo Bay and writing his own programs and websites. Amias loves being creative and challenging himself with new problems and things to learn. Whilst working towards a job as an engineer, Amias took a Computer Science course in college. He found that after two years he had developed a fondness of the enigmas computers and everything he now found he could do with computers he decided to take computing up as a hobby of his. With a new hobby playing in his spare time Amias decided to take another two years of college doing a Level 3 Music Practitioner course. This was the spark that re-ignited a passion from his childhood and where he found his current band members of his band Indigo Bay. On this course he discovered his love for working in studios.


Present day Amias has filled his spare time learning new skills forking from these two pillars of his life. If you want to see his work you'll find his music on his Portfolio Page and his programming progress by using the Contact form at the bottom of the page. Feel free to contact him if you have a question or wish to just chat to him.
